Tentang Sistem — BRD v1.3

Ringkasan dari docs/BRD-Contract-Management-System-v1.3.md (Document Control: 2026-07-08).

Objective (BRD §3)

Menyediakan Contract Management System yang tersentralisasi, terkontrol, dan tertelusuri untuk administrasi kontrak & monitoring lifecycle oleh Legal dan stakeholder terkait — struktur record party-centric di Odoo, dengan integrasi Sales Order & Partner Master.

  • Party & contract records tersentralisasi
  • Search Party / Contract criteria + smart search
  • Add contract + extract/index metadata
  • Edit metadata administratif (field sensitif terkunci)
  • Review status + e-sign / manual sign
  • Change CP / Amendment / Early Termination (Legal-managed, tanpa approval)
  • History audit di konteks Party & Contract
  • SO sync dari Odoo + renewal calendar
  • Party Master ↔ Odoo Partner link/relink
  • RBAC + audit trail lengkap

In Scope (16 item · §4.1)

  • 1–2. Login/Dashboard, Party-level Search & View
  • 3–5. Add Contract, Edit Contract Details, Review Status
  • 6–8. Change Counterparty, Amendment/Addendum, Early Termination
  • 9–10. Upload Supporting Document, SO Synchronization
  • 11–13. Parties Search & View, Audit Trail, Notifications
  • 14–16. Parties: Add / Edit / Delete-Deactivate

Out of Scope (10 item · §4.2)

  • Automatic Odoo Partner Master creation
  • Legal approval workflow (CP/Amendment/Termination Legal-managed)
  • Contract clause negotiation automation
  • Automatic contract drafting dari template
  • Certified legal advice / legal risk scoring
  • Odoo SO creation/amendment (hanya konsumsi data)
  • Invoice, billing, payment, AR/AP processing
  • E-sign provider commercial setup
  • Historical contract migration (bulk)
  • Advanced analytics / BI dashboard eksternal

Stakeholders (BRD §5)

Legal / Contract Admin — full access Business User / Requestor — view + request Counterparty — review & sign eksternal Signer — tanda tangan manual/e-sign Finance / Commercial — view referensi Management / Directors — view-only IT / Odoo Support — konfigurasi & akses Odoo ERP / SO Module — sumber data sistem E-Sign Provider — sistem eksternal

Login prototype mensimulasikan 5 role internal (Legal, Business User, Finance, Management, IT). Counterparty, Signer, dan sistem eksternal (Odoo, E-Sign Provider) berinteraksi lewat kanal terpisah sesuai BRD §5 / §12, bukan lewat login CMS ini. Dokumen lengkap: docs/BRD-Contract-Management-System-v1.3.md.
